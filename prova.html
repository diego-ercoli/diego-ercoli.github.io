About me

I’ve recently completed a Master’s Degree in Computer Science at University of Turin.

During my studies, I had the chance to work in an international company as a Junior Data Engineer/Scientist . Then I was in charge of developing my Master’s Thesis in DataScience at Links Foundation, which allows me to develop deep knowledge about statistics and time series analysis/forecasting using both Python and R. After my graduation, I have worked in a Turin-based company for Edison customer.
